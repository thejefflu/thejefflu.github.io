About

Hey! My name is Jeff.
I'm a sophomore at UCLA studying Cognitive Science and Linguistics of Computer Science. My mouthful of a degree plan was inspired, in large part, by my fascination with the psychology behind beauty and aesthetics. Since I am also a longtime proponent of Marie Kondo, I believe that good design has the power to create a world that sparks more joy!

I'm currently a UI/UX designer at TransCanWork, helping advance workpace inclusion by connecting trans and gender non-conforming job seekers with resources to find employment. Outside of UI/UX, I also have a background in UX engineering, full stack development, and marketing/print design.

When I'm not designing things, I can probably be spotted hosting a board game night, solving the daily NYT crossword, or learning any instrument I can get my hands on (I'm at 5 and counting)!
