Hey! My name is Jeff.

I'm a sophomore at UCLA studying Cognitive Science and Linguistics of Computer Science. My mouthful of a degree plan was chosen as part of my journey to explore the psychology behind beauty and aesthetics. As a longtime proponent of the teachings of Marie Kondo, I believe that good design has the power to create a world that sparks more joy!

Throughout my career, I've delivered projects for startups, non-profit organizations, businesses, and more. I'm currently a UI/UX engineer at TransCanWork, helping advance workplace inclusion by connecting trans jobseekers with resources to find employment.

When I'm not designing things, I can probably be spotted hosting a board game night, solving the daily NYT crossword, or learning any instrument I can get my hands on (I'm at 6 and counting)!
