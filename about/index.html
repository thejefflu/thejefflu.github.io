Hey! My name is Jeff.

I'm a junior at UCLA studying Cognitive Science and Linguistics of Computer Science. Besides the fact that it makes me sound cool and smart, what I love about my combination of majors is that it represents what design is to me: a contemplation of what makes us human, and the constantly evolving technologies we use to express it.

If my major didn't give it away, I like to think of myself as a jack of all trades. From my pre-designer days, I have a background in software engineering and orchestral performance. But as much as I love making music and websites (like this one!), I am a perfectionist at heart: a trait that's frustrating for a programmer, borderline self-destructive for a musician.

Thankfully, I found a home in design: a place where I could leverage my perfectionism to create a beautiful bigger picture, while also tending to the small but impactful details that drive delightful experiences. As a longtime proponent of the philosophy of Marie Kondo, I believe that good design has the power to create a world that sparks more joy!

When I'm not designing things, I can probably be spotted hosting a board game night, solving the daily NYT crossword, or learning any instrument I can get my hands on (I'm at 6 and counting!)
